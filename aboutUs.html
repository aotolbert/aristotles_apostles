About Us Overview

Inspired by our love for politics, this site was created to assist users discover their federal state senators and house of representatives. Our state and local politicians are the symbols and voices for our communities, so it is extremely important to stay up to date with who is representing your community and how they are looking out for your best interest. Combining the power of Google Maps, ProPublica, and Open-States, this site provides each user with an abundance of information regarding their state and local politicians.
